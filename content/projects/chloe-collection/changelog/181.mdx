# v1.81

- Added 2 new high-quality cars: Honda Civic, Honda S2000
- Added a text popup after entering cheat codes, showing what content you've unlocked or what you've enabled
- Added new cheats to unlock hidden content
- Added support for teamplay in multiplayer: As the host, enter `/teams true` in the chat, players can then choose their team by using `/team (1/2/3/4/red/blue/green/yellow)`
- Fixed the Map Veteran achievements not being trackable and showing up as 0% complete until you finish a race
- Player names in multiplayer are now colored and get grayed out if you lap someone
